## 🚀 About Us

Welcome to the official GitHub repository hub for **ACES BVCOE**! We are a passionate, student-led community dedicated to fostering technical excellence, critical thinking, and collaborative development. Our goal is to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry standards through real-world projects, workshops, and hackathons.

- 💻 We organize events, coding challenges, and tech workshops.
- 🌐 We actively build platforms to support our students and college ecosystem.
- 🤝 We believe in learning by breaking, building, and sharing!
