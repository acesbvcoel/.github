## 🚀 What is ACES?

The **Association of Computer Engineering Students (ACES)** was established on September 15, 2014, at Bharati Vidyapeeth’s College of Engineering, Lavale, Pune. We are a vibrant community of over 300 passionate tech enthusiasts, student software developers, and faculty members dedicated to pushing the boundaries of technology.

We believe in **learning by breaking, building, and sharing**. Our core mission is to enhance the overall educational experience and personal development of our members by bridging the gap between academia and the rapidly evolving tech industry.

---

## 🎯 Our Focus & Activities

We are actively involved in designing, developing, and deploying open-source projects, college utility platforms, and modern web applications. ACES is committed to creating meaningful technical experiences for our students through:

- 💻 **Hackathons & Coding Competitions:** Challenging our members to solve real-world problems.
- 🛠️ **Workshops & Seminars:** Hands-on sessions covering the latest in AI, Web Dev, UI/UX, and more.
- 🎤 **Guest Lectures & Industry Connects:** Bringing industry experts and startup leaders directly to the campus.
- 🤝 **Collaborative Development:** Building real software systems, like our official portal, through teamwork.
